Company

The best solution to dent repair, alternative to the traditional way.

The history

An intuition starts the engine of the human machine: a company with a solid base in the repairing damaged cars field is the first to see the signs of change. 

  • 1977

    TRADITIONAL BODY
    Ballsystem is set up, a small body shop that moves the first steps in the traditional repair shop field. 

  • 1990

    DAMAGED VEHICLES
    The body shop grows and the know-how acquired makes the company an expert in car repair, especially cars damaged in accidents.

  • 1997

    PAINTLESS DENT REPAIR
    The strong innovativeness leads to years of great experimentation, which result in a revolutionary technique: cold intervention applied on vehicles damaged by hail, no need of painting. 

  • 2005

    BALLSYSTEM ACADEMY
    Establishing the Ballsystem Academy and the Research and Development Centre opens up new horizons: training and advanced technology become key pillars in the company philosophy and bring about two new repairing approaches: Smart and Spot Repair.

  • 2010

    BALLSYSTEM NETWORK
    The philosophy and the techniques of Ballsystem become a system of values and a reference model. Many small enterprises join the company, creating the first Ballsystem Network. It has a registered capital of €100,000.000 fully paid up.

  • 2013

    GREENPARTNER POINT
    The network is called Greenpartner and it is significantly spread throughout Italy and the rest of the world, with a number of activities and advanced methods.

  • 2015

    BALLSYSTEM SPA
    Ambition and precision at work lead the registered capital to increase to €1,350,000 and the company becomes a joint stock company. 

  • 2019

    BALLSYSTEM PATENT
    After the technical instruments used, also the Ballsystem method is officially patented and the company is credited as a training centre for the activity of the Academy.

  • 2020

    SOLE INTERLOCUTOR
    The all-round work of Ballsystem expands the core business, and the company offers comprehensive automotive services. A unique interlocutor in the sector able to meet any market needs.
