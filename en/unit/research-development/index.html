Research & Development

An active centre for research, testing and final development, that comes full circle on the ability of Ballsystem to create internally its own work-mates.

The tools, patented by Ballsystem are key elements of our repair system: they come from our practical approach and from brilliant intuitions, to meet bigger and bigger challenges, in the most effective and efficient way, without giving up on environmental protection.

Each instrument is long tested  by engineers and technicians who assess the quality at work. The department Research and Development  is the beating heart that constantly drives Ballsystem evolution.
