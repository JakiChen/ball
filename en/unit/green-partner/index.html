Greenpartner

Man at the centre: this has been for Ballsystem the starting point to imagine and to build a new model of network based on the culture of training and the continuous transfer of know-how.

To be Greenpartner means to be part of an open network, where the systems become complementary: in full entrepreneurial autonomy the panel beater integrates the Ballsystem method in their work making it more competitive.

With the expertise developed by a structured company like Ballsystem and digital monitoring technologies, the individual reality can work for a continuous improvement of the performance totally respecting the timing of the repairer.
