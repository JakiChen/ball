Experience that enables growth

Ballsystem has long decided to systematize their knowledge to be the mouthpiece of a real change, to create a sustainable future and to help those who believe in human and technical values start new paths for their work.
Through the Ballsystem Academy, the company develops training courses to become professionals Greenpartners of excellence in using the method and the repair tools patented by the brand. Acknowledging this work across all of the network, Ballsystem SPA was officially accredited by Regione Umbria as a Training centre.- code7697, for planning and providing training according to the regional law of 2 March 2005 n.397.( published in BURU 9th March 2005). As a result of this, the company is allowed to do the following:

  1. Presenting training projects that are eligible for subsidies from ESF;
  2. Proposing training courses in the Catalogue of the Regional Training Offer;
  3. Issuing certificates of professional skills according to the article 14 law 845/78.

A commitment Ballsystem carries on aiming at a future characterized by carefulness, technical quality and sustainability, all in a network and a hallmark of excellence.
