We said yes

After years-long collaboration and respect, Ballsystem makes the partnership with Bureau Veritas official.

Ballsystem has long been creating solid relationships with excellent partners in the supply chain. Continuative collaborations that speed up management and simplify activities bringing processes to one common workflow, established to respond to the quick demands of the market players and of the final customer.

The partnership with Bureau Veritas, world leader in intervention of inspection, compliance check and clearance, that has developed a specific portfolio for the automotive sector, arises from the desire to take this relationship to a new level, improving even more the range of services of Ballsystem.

‘The quality achieved in these years working along with Bureau Veritas has a great value for us’, says Antonello Fiorenza, General Manager of Ballsystem , ‘and now it is ready to take on a new shape that moves our relation to a new level. Knowing that you can take the quality of your work to higher and higher standards is stimulating and it is fundamental to officialize this step with a company that embraces the same company vision as ours and wants to continue growing. With Bureau Veritas we feel we are looking at the same future, which is going to be much more beautiful for the whole market chain’.

‘We are very satisfied of the partnership with such a prestigious and competent reality as Ballsystem’, says Simone Dore, Automotive Manager Italy Bureau Veritas, ‘and we are sure that together we will further increase the quality standards, providing customers more and more performing services, in an ever advancing market, where we are always in the front line in offering cutting-edge solutions’.

A process of exchanging and sharing competences, technologies, platforms, know-how and logistics that frames the common best practice to use in each phase of the work. Better organization, higher speed in execution, higher quality and a guarantee that certificates the excellence of a state of art service: these are the fundamental points and results of a partnership absolutely beneficial to both the final customer and the business. The latter can rely on a technical interlocutor, skilled, qualified, authoritative and solid. Together with the great human machine of Ballsystem, it offers a thorough and complete job from the first contact to the after-care.


The common vision of Ballsystem and Bureau Veritas opens the doors to new horizons and to a new meaning of excellence.
