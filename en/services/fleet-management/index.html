Fleet operations

Ballsystem Fleet operations are a series of prompt actions carried out managing and restoring large fleet, cars for short-term and long-term rental and car parks.

  • Observation and assessment

    The car fleet is carefully observed to assess the state of use and elaborate the methods and specific strategies for intervention.

  • Buy back management

    Ballsystem manages the flow of buy back with a modular approach: decades of experience have lead the company to create a series of packages, promptly exportable from fleet to fleet, depending on needs.

     

  • Fleet Authority

    In the event of damage to fleet, the Authority on the report is managed by Ballsystem in a global way, starting from the estimate.
