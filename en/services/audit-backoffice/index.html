Audit

The Audit service checks on the correctness of the activities and creates monitoring systems to improve the company effectiveness.

  • Observatory

    It is the monitoring centre of the specific market, forecast, survey and analysis of changes. It allows speedy reaction to changes and to new business opportunities.

  • Sales and assistance network

    The sales and assistance service to the customer constantly monitors the progress of the activity.

     

  • Back-office and on-field

    The operation management monitors the production system and includes intervention on site with car manufacturers, repair network and dealers. 

     

  • Counseling

    Qualified personnel provides counseling tailored on the customer’s need, about certifications, procedures and method of repair.
